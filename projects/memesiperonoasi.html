ABOUT

Challenging Extremism Estuio 7

Design of social interaction for conflict management. Research on the forms of violence present in social networks within the University of the Andes. It was found that through memes on social networks, bullying was being carried out and expressing ideas of racism, classism, among others.
From this research, a series of activities was proposed to raise awareness in the community. I was one of the people in charge of creating one of the activities that focuses on dialogue. This activity is replicated every year at the university.
